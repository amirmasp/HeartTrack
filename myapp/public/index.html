Measurement

Track & Collect your heart rate and blood oxygen saturation level measurements using a heart rate and oxygen sensor.

Summary & Monitor

The IoT device will transmit measurements to a web application through which users can view their data. Supports a weekly summary view and detailed day view.

Security

Users can create/update an account and register their devices easily. All data will be encrypted and stored in a database to ensure that user accounts are protected and properly secured.
